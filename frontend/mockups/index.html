Prompting as choreographic search · v0 interface mockups

BodyPrompt

A practice-based artistic research project investigating prompting as a choreographic search — an iterative dialogue in which human intention and generative AI co-evolve toward movement that resonates with a poetic theme. Generated movement is shown as stick figures and notation, not realistic avatars, to foreground movement itself as the material of inquiry. These screens are the research instruments.

Makes generated movement readable and comparable — four notation registers — without relying on realistic human appearance.

Performance mode

Supports live collaborative search between performer, audience and AI during a lecture performance — a readable score to re-embody, lineage growing live.
